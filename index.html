Short history of graffiti

The history of graffiti and street art originated in Philadelphia in 1967, when high school students started to spray paint in the city to get the attention of a girl. Over time, graffiti paint spread all over US, usually in inaccessible public places, as a small competition between artists. Most of them were teenagers, each with their own original style of artwork. They used these methods to denounce society and its evolution, and used public locations as a place to be seen and heard.
